Research

During undergraduates, I had one year as a visiting student at NTU, Singapore where I worked with Dr. Thinh Pham on a hardware acceleration project for ORB object detection in self-driving cars system. As an engineer in Fujitsu-SMU Corp. Lab, I developed a deep learning model for incidents predictions and a routing engine for vehicle schedulings.

I am interested in computer vision and reinforcement learning for robotic manipulations. In particularly, I want to study an end-to-end vision-based policy that can learn tasks autonomously and generalize to new tasks without human supervisions.
