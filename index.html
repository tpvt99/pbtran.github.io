Research

During undergraduates, I had one year as a visiting student at NTU, Singapore where I worked with Professor Siew Kei Lam and Dr. Thinh Pham on a hardware acceleration project for ORB object detection in self-driving cars system. As an engineer in Fujitsu-SMU Corp. Lab, I developed a deep learning model for incidents predictions and a routing engine for vehicle schedulings.

I am interested in autonomous driving, especially in motion prediction and planning. My current research focuses on planning under uncertainty by formulating the problem as a POMDP. I would like to explore the potential of multimodal Language Models (LM) to understand the world through vision and devise interpretable, safe and robust policy for autonomous driving.
