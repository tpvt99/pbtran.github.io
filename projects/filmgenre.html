Film Genre Prediction

Sashankh Chengavalli Kumar, Tran Phong, Wong Wei Hao

CS5242 Neural Networks and Deep Learning

National University of Singapore, Singapore

Introduction
Neural Networks have demonstrated remarkable success in numerous Natural Language Processing tasks. This project embarks on a journey to explore deep learning approaches for text classification, specifically predicting the film genre based on its plot. Through the utilization of Wikipedia APIs for dataset preparation, rigorous data cleaning, and experimentation with various neural networks, the project aims to develop an efficient and accurate system for film genre prediction.
